How It Works

This tool uses a technique called **frame blending** to create new, intermediate video frames. It's a simple yet effective form of interpolation.

  1. **Frame Extraction:** The app carefully extracts every frame from your uploaded video.
  2. **Blending:** For each pair of consecutive frames, a new frame is generated by averaging the pixel colors of the two original frames.
  3. **Reassembly:** The original frames and the new, blended frames are stitched back together into a new video with double the frame rate.

This process happens entirely in your browser. Your video is never uploaded to a server.
